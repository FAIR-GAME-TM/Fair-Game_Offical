About Us

At Fair Game, we believe that everyone deserves a fair chance to succeed. That's why we're committed to providing equal access to clothing creators, while also giving back to schools in need through our Scholar Garments program. With our unique approach to fashion, we're making a positive impact on both the industry and our community.

Attendees will have hands-on experience discovering and utilizing knowledge found in Scholar Garments via exciting activities including screen printing, dyeing, fashion design, and graphic design.

Fair-Game seeks to provide access to resources, information, and opportunities in fashion, art, and business. We advocate for equal opportunities for individuals, regardless of their circumstances or background.

Our, “Scholar Garments” represent a distinct fashion trend that goes beyond simple apparel and is primarily focused on knowledge-sharing and education.
